    GENERAL INSTRUCTIONS

    Overview

    Knowing how to properly use a disposable vape is crucial for several reasons. Following manufacturer's instructions ensures safe handling, minimizing the risk of accidents or harm. Proper usage also maximizes the device's effectiveness and lifespan, ensuring a satisfactory vaping experience. Additionally, understanding disposal procedures is essential for environmental protection and safety. Proper storage techniques preserve the device's integrity, preventing damage or malfunction. Adhering to guidelines for usage, disposal, and storage promotes safety, efficiency, and environmental responsibility in vaping practices.

    If you plan to store your disposable vapes for a LONG TIME, it is essential to consider the duration of use. To showcase optimal performance, it's generally advisable to use the tool within a reasonable time after initiating it. Therefore, disposable vapes, are designed for immediate use and may exhibit a limited shelf life post-use. So, see the manufacturer's recommendations for specific instructions on the ideal use of disposable vapes. Also, suppose you do not plan to use your disposable vape for an extended period. In that case, it may be beneficial to store your disposable vape in an unused state to preserve battery life and e-liquid freshness.


    Before using a disposable vape

    • Read the manufacturer's instructions: Before attempting to open a disposable vape pen, it is recommended to check the manufacturer's instructions. They may provide specific guidance on how to safely open or dispose of the device.
    • Inspect the Device: Ensure that the packaging is intact and there are no leaks. If you notice any issues, do not use the device, do not break the seal and contact us.
    • Shake (if recommended): Some disposable vapes, particularly those with nicotine salts or thicker e-liquids, may benefit from shaking before use. This helps ensure that the e-liquid is well-mixed and distributed evenly. Shake the device gently for a atleast 10-15 seconds.
    • Dispose of the device properly: If you are unable to open the disposable vape pen or if you have finished using it, it is crucial to dispose of it properly. Follow the manufacturer's instructions or local regulations for safe disposal.

    How to properly store disposable vapes

    • Magnetic Fields: DO NOT store disposable vapes near magnets often found in speakers, electronic devices, or metal. Despite their small size, these devices have negative electric currents and electric currents that are affected by magnetic fields.
    • Temperature: High temperatures can cause the e-liquid to degrade and leak, while low temperatures can affect the battery performance. It is recommended to store disposable vapes at room temperature.
    • Avoid Direct Sunlight: Exposing disposable vapes to direct sunlight can damage the device and alter the flavor of the e-liquid.
    • Humidity: High humidity levels can cause moisture to seep into the device. It is advisable to store disposable vapes in a dry environment to prevent humidity-related issues.
    • Safe and Secure: Ensure that your chosen storage location is safe and secure, especially if you have children or pets.
    • Handling Precautions: Avoid placing heavy objects on top of them or exposing them to excessive pressure, as this can lead to device damage.
    • Upright Position: Storing your disposable vape upright helps prevent leakage, keeps its cotton saturated, avoids airflow issues, and prevents damage to internal components.
